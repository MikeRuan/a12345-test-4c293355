My Favourite Hobby:
Drawing & Art!

A webpage made with ❤️ by a Grade 5 student

🧒

👋 Hi, I'm Alex!

I'm 10 years old and I'm in Grade 5. My absolute favourite thing to do after school is drawing and making art. I love creating pictures of animals, fantasy worlds, and my friends. Art makes me feel happy and calm, and I've been drawing since I was just 4 years old! 🌟

⭐ My 3 Favourite Things About Drawing ⭐

🦄

Drawing Animals

My favourite thing to draw is fantasy animals like unicorns and dragons. I love making up their colours and patterns!

🖍️

Trying New Tools

I love experimenting with pencils, markers, watercolours, and even digital drawing on a tablet. Each tool feels different!

📖

Making Comics

I make my own comic strips with funny characters. My best comic is about a superhero cat named Captain Whiskers! 🐱

💜 Why I Like Drawing

  • 😌 It relaxes me after a busy day at school — drawing feels like a mini-vacation for my brain!
  • 🌍 I can create my own worlds and characters that exist only in my imagination.
  • 🤝 My friends love it when I draw pictures of them as superheroes — it makes everyone smile!
  • 🏆 I entered a school art contest last year and won second place — that made me so proud!
  • 🚀 One day I want to be a game designer or comic artist — drawing is my first step!

🌟 Fun Fact About Me 🌟

I have filled up 5 whole sketchbooks this year alone — that's over 300 drawings! 📚✏️
